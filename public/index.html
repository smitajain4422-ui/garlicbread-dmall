How to Use

Quick guide to running your first successful DMall.

Welcome to the Multi Bot DMall. Follow these steps to get started:

  • 1. Add Tokens: Go to the Config tab. Paste your bot tokens and organize them into folders (e.g. "Server 1 Bots"). Click 'Check' to ensure they are alive.
  • 2. Build your Embed: Stay in Config. Paste a raw Discord JSON embed into the Embed Builder. Use the Live Preview to make sure it looks correct.
  • 3. Set Delays: Lower delays are faster, but higher delays prevent your bots from getting rate-limited or banned.
  • 4. Launch: Go to the Home tab, paste the Server ID you want to target, select your bot folder and embed, and click Start!
