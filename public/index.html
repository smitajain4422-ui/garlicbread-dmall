📖 Guide

1. Tokens: Import Bot tokens in the Config tab. Name your folder to keep things organized.

2. Profile: Expand your folder and use "Mass Edit" to quickly set avatars and names for your bots.

3. Message: Create your message JSON in the Embed Builder and save it.

4. Launch: Go to the Start tab, select your folder and target server. Hit Start.

Your campaign runs entirely on our 24/7 secure background engine. You can safely close your browser or turn off your computer.
