Comprehensive Guide

Mastering the Nosify DMall Engine.

1. Managing Tokens & Mass Upload

In the Config tab, paste your Discord bot tokens. You can paste a single token, a list of tokens separated by newlines, or a raw JSON array. The engine handles all of them. Organize them into "Folders". Click Check to ensure they aren't terminated.

2. Live Webhook Previewer

We use Discord Webhooks to test exactly how your embed looks without risking a bot token. Create a Webhook in a private server, paste its URL into the Config tab, paste your raw JSON (V2 components perfectly supported), and hit Send Test to see it live in Discord.

3. Auto-Leave & Delays

Auto-Leave: Toggle this on in the Home tab if you want your bots to nuke themselves from the server immediately after they finish sending DMs to prevent server admins from reporting them.
Delays: Adjust the Matrix variables. 200ms is default. If you get 403 errors, raise it to 500-1000ms.
